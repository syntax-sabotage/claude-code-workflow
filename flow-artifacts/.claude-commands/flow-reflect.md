---
description: Stream Reflection
---

Conduct reflection on completed stream "$ARGUMENTS":

1. Read `.flow/streams/active/$ARGUMENTS.md` (or completed/)
2. Review the full stream history:
   - Original objective and success criteria
   - All completed objectives
   - Learnings captured along the way
   - Decisions made
3. Generate reflection:
   - What went well?
   - What was harder than expected?
   - What would we do differently?
   - What patterns emerged that should be documented?
4. Propose updates to `.context/` docs:
   - New patterns for architecture/patterns.md?
   - New anti-patterns discovered?
   - Glossary updates?
   - Rule changes?
5. Move stream file to `streams/completed/`
6. Update metrics with final stream data
7. Present reflection summary for review
